# Risk Analysis

| Transaction | Risk | Business Impact | Priority |
|---|---|---|---|
Checkout | Incorrect total calculation | Revenue loss | High |
Payment | Payment success but transaction not recorded | Financial loss | Critical |
Payment | Duplicate payment charge | Customer complaints | High |
Refund | Multiple refunds for same transaction | Financial loss | High |
Inventory Update | Inventory not reduced after sale | Inventory mismatch | High |
Add Item to Cart | Incorrect quantity added | Cart total incorrect | Medium |
Barcode Scan | Scanner fails to detect barcode | Slower checkout process | Low |
